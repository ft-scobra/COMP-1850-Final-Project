Tier Explaination

Explaination for Roles

DPS Main DPS characters are those who spend the majority of their time on the battlefield, using their attacks to deal damage.
Sub-DPS
  • Sub-DPS characters contribute supplementary damage through their skills.
  • Many Sub-DPS characters can also apply elementals on enemies for the Main DPS to trigger.
Support
  • Supports primarily serve as buffers for the team, enhancing overall damage output through their skills.
  • Additionally, they contribute to the team's quality of life (QoL) by offering crowd control, healing, shields, or taunting abilities, making battles more manageable.
